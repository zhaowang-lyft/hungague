# Privacy Policy — Hunger Gauge

**Last updated:** March 2026

## What data we collect

Hunger Gauge reads dish names, restaurant names, and order dates from the HungerHub Uncatering website so you can rate your meals. Your ratings, star scores, and comments are stored locally in your browser using `chrome.storage.local`.

## What we don't collect

- No personally identifiable information
- No analytics or tracking
- No cookies
- No data sent to external servers

## Data sharing

We do not sell, transfer, or share any user data with third parties. All data stays on your device.

## Data removal

Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data. You can also clear ratings at any time through the extension.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
